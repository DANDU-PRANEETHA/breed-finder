Breed Finder

An app that can identify the breed of a pet, and provide information about its characteristics.
Pet owners can also use it to share photos and connect with other pet owners.
Animal shelters and rescues can use it to identify the breeds of rescued animals.


About Home Services


Comprehensive Breed Database:
Dive into a vast repository of dog breeds, each accompanied by detailed descriptions of their characteristics, temperament, exercise requirements, and grooming needs. Our comprehensive database ensures you have access to the information necessary for making an informed decision about your future four-legged family member.

Personalized Matching Algorithm:
Our advanced algorithm takes into account your living situation, activity level, available space, and preferences to provide you with a tailored list of breeds that are a perfect match for your unique lifestyle. Say goodbye to guesswork and hello to a personalized selection process.


Visual Galleries:
Explore stunning visual galleries for each breed, featuring high-quality images showcasing their distinct appearance, size, and coat variations. Get a firsthand look at the charm and personality of each breed, making it easier for you to envision life with your potential new companion.


Interactive Quizzes:
Enjoy a fun and interactive experience with our breed-specific quizzes. Discover more about your preferences, and let our quizzes guide you towards breeds that align with your expectations. It's a playful way to learn more about yourself and your ideal canine companion.


Local Adoption and Breeder Connect:
Once you've identified your preferred breeds, easily connect with reputable local breeders or adoption centers. We prioritize responsible and ethical breeding practices to ensure the well-being of the animals and facilitate seamless connections between prospective owners and breeders.


Educational Resources:
Equip yourself with valuable insights into dog care, training tips, and health considerations. Our application provides a wealth of educational resources to help you prepare for the responsibilities of dog ownership and ensure a happy and healthy life for your new pet.


Bookmark and Share:
Save your favorite breeds for future reference and share your discoveries with friends and family. Our user-friendly interface allows you to bookmark breeds, making it convenient to revisit and compare your top choices as you embark on your journey to find the perfect companion.
